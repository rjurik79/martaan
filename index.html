ABOUT ME

 My name is Marco and I am a Web - Graphic Designer located in Milan, Italy. I like simple and friendly interfaces and easy graphic design creations.

I managed also the Release Management Software process at Fastweb s.p.a and create technical documentation and reports in word and excel about the activities and architecture systems involved. About two years ago I decide to change my profesional profile and , after two profesional courses, started to work as Graphic and Web Designer free lance. Actually I’m working as copywriter and graphic designer at Key-One company in Milan.
